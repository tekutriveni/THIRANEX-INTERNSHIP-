Design Improvements

8 Improvement Recommendations

Actionable design improvements based on usability testing findings, prioritized by impact.

1
Saved Addresses & GPS Auto-detect
Finding: Checkout form too long & tedious
High
Add saved delivery addresses and GPS auto-detect for location. This directly addresses the #1 pain point that caused a 20% checkout drop-off rate.
CheckoutUX FlowSprint 1
2
Cart Micro-animation & Badge Count
Finding: No visual feedback on Add to Cart
High
Add subtle bounce animation on cart icon + update badge count immediately when item is added. Low effort, high impact on user confidence.
Micro-interactionCartSprint 1
3
Redesigned Payment Options
Finding: Payment options not differentiated
High
Use distinct icons, brand colors, and clear labels for UPI, Card, and COD options. Add a "Recommended" tag on the most popular payment method.
PaymentVisual DesignSprint 1
4
Pinned Search Bar on Home
Finding: Search bar not prominent enough
Medium
Pin search bar permanently to top of Home Screen. 40% of users expected to search before browsing. This aligns with Swiggy & Zomato patterns.
Home ScreenNavigationSprint 2
5
Larger Category Filter Tabs
Finding: Category filters missed by 40% users
Medium
Increase tap target size of category tabs, add active state highlight color, and ensure they're visible without scrolling on the Home Screen.
Home ScreenFiltersSprint 2
6
Back Navigation Interactions
Finding: No back button in prototype
Medium
Add back button prototype connections on all screens. This improves User Control & Freedom (Nielsen Heuristic #3) and reduces user frustration.
NavigationPrototypeSprint 2
7
Order Review Before Payment
Finding: No order review step
Medium
Add mandatory order summary screen before final payment — shows items, quantities, delivery address, and total. Reduces accidental orders and increases confidence.
CheckoutError PreventionSprint 2
8
Error & Empty States
Finding: No error states shown in prototype
Low
Design error states for search no-results, empty cart, network error, and failed payment. These are critical for a production-ready app experience.
Error StatesEdge CasesSprint 3
